PRODUCT PHOTO STANDARDS



The following rules are part of our reviewing process. Please note that failing to meet these standards could be a reason to reject your participation request.

Rule #1: Good Lighting

Go and take those bad boys into the sunlight!

Rule #2: Neutral or Cleared or Separated background

This always makes all the difference! A clean wall or floor would definitely suffice ☺️

Rule #3: One product per picture

Make sure to not confuse your customers by only taking one product per post

Rule #4: No blurry pictures

It’s just a no.
